Long-Term Rental (LTR) Analysis

Underwriting includes rent comps, vacancy, expense load, CapEx/maintenance, and financing terms (cash, hard money, or creative financing). We provide pro‑forma cash flow, DSCR, break‑even, and sensitivity to rent/interest assumptions.

Outcome: Clear hold strategy with projected cash‑on‑cash, equity growth, and risk notes.

Mid-Term Rental (MTR) Analysis

Mid-Term Rental (MTR) strategies focus on 30-90 day stays, targeting traveling professionals, corporate housing, and extended stay guests. Our analysis evaluates furnished rental premiums, seasonal demand patterns, and operational requirements for successful MTR operations.

Market Analysis: Corporate demand assessment, seasonal pricing strategies, competition analysis, and operational cost evaluation including furnishing, utilities, and enhanced property management requirements.

Short-Term Rental (STR) Analysis

Short-Term Rental (STR) analysis examines the vacation rental market potential, including Airbnb and VRBO performance metrics. We evaluate local regulations, seasonal demand, and revenue optimization strategies to maximize your property's earning potential in the short-term rental market.

Revenue Optimization: Dynamic pricing strategies, occupancy rate projections, guest experience enhancement, and compliance with local STR regulations and licensing requirements.

Fix & Flip analysis

Fix & Flip analysis provides comprehensive renovation cost estimates, after-repair value (ARV) calculations, and timeline projections. We evaluate market demand for renovated properties and identify the optimal scope of work to maximize profit margins while minimizing holding costs.

Project Management: Detailed renovation budgets, contractor vetting, timeline optimization, and exit strategy planning to ensure profitable and timely project completion with maximum return on investment.
